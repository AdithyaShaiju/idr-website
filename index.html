Who We Are

Built at the intersection
of academia and practice

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to building the specialist talent that regulated, high-consequence sectors urgently need. We bring together the analytical depth of leading UK university partners with the operational experience of practitioners who have navigated real-world cyber, digital and technology risk environments.

Our model moves talent from the classroom directly into consequential roles. Trainees work on live projects before entering the workforce — giving employers confidence and giving practitioners the applied credentials that accelerate their careers.

Service Pillars

Three ways we deliver

From foundational education to cutting-edge advisory, IDR's three pillars work together to close the digital risk talent gap.

Pillar 01

Academy

Structured training programmes and intensive bootcamps for students, graduates and working professionals looking to build or advance digital and cyber risk capabilities.

  • Digital & Cyber Risk Bootcamps
  • AI Risk & Governance Pathways
  • Continuing Professional Development
  • University-accredited modules

Pillar 02

Innovation & Incubation

Working with academic and industry partners to develop proprietary risk models, AI governance tools and emerging frameworks that keep organisations ahead of the threat landscape.

  • IP development & risk modelling
  • AI risk & safety research
  • Future risk foresight projects
  • Industry-academic co-creation

Pillar 03

Advisory Services

Hands-on advisory support helping organisations design, implement and mature their digital risk frameworks in line with leading international standards and regulatory obligations.

  • NIST CSF implementation
  • ISO 27001 & NIS2 alignment
  • Organisational risk maturity
  • Board-level risk communication
